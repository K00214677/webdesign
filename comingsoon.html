What's coming soon to Nintendo 4U?

Splatoon 2 Global Testfire Demo

Over the weekend, Nintendo gave Switch players an exclusive look at Splatoon 2's online mode. You can look forward to my review on it soon but for now, please take a look at some gameplay footage.

Splatoon 2 will release on Switch in full 1080p HD at 60fps both online and offline this Summer, 2017. Stay Fresh!


Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

Originally released on Wii U in 2014, Nintendo is bringing the definitive version of Mario Kart 8 to the Nintendo Switch. The game will run docked in 1080p at 60fps and will feature everything from the original game and more!

I can't wait for the release of this game along with all the previous DLC for free when it releases April 24th only on Nintendo Switch.
