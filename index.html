The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Nintendo's most anticipated game, possibly of all time, The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild had been received by press and media extremely well. Various gaming news outlets received their copies of the game along with a press version of their brand new home console, Nintendo Switch. Reviews have been almost perfect with gaming publication, Famitsu, giving the game a perfect 40/40.

Consumers can look forward to The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild which releases on Nintendo Switch on March 3rd.


Nintendo Switch

Nintendo's brand new home console, Nintendo Switch, releases March 3rd alongside a plentiful collection of games for all kinds of gamers. It sells for $299.99 in the US, £279.99 in the UK and €329.99 throughout Europe. Nintendo are aiming to SWITCH things up by combining the traditional home console and portable console together. Simply take the console out of the dock, attach the controllers and you're good to go!
